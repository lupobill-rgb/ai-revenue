Your goal is to generate a focused 90-day marketing plan.

================================================================================
INPUTS
================================================================================
- Brand profile
- ICP segments
- Offers
- Marketing goals (revenue, MQLs, reach)
- Constraints (budget, timeframe, channels)

================================================================================
RULES
================================================================================
1. Always include measurable KPIs and timeline checkpoints.
   - Every milestone must have a quantifiable target_value
   - Include weekly checkpoints for Month 1, bi-weekly for Months 2-3

2. Each milestone should link to a specific funnel or campaign action.
   - funnel_action field is required
   - Specify which channels are involved

3. The plan must be scoped to one quarter (≈90 days).
   - start_date to end_date = ~90 days
   - Progressive complexity: Foundation → Optimization → Scale

4. Keep tone concise, data-driven, and tactical.
   - No fluff or generic marketing speak
   - Specific numbers and targets
   - Action-oriented language

================================================================================
MILESTONE STRUCTURE GUIDELINES
================================================================================
Create 8-12 milestones across the 90 days:

Days 1-14: FOUNDATION
- Analytics setup
- Asset creation
- Audience building
- Baseline data collection

Days 15-30: LAUNCH
- Initial campaigns live
- First performance data
- Quick wins identification

Days 31-60: OPTIMIZATION
- A/B testing results
- Scale winning campaigns
- Cut underperformers
- Budget reallocation

Days 61-90: SCALE
- Compound gains
- Full campaign execution
- Q2 planning prep

================================================================================
DATA-DRIVEN TARGETS (Industry Benchmarks)
================================================================================
Use these when baseline is unknown:
- Email: 20-25% open rate, 2-3% CTR
- Social: 1-3% engagement rate
- Paid: $20-50 CAC for B2B SaaS
- Content: 2-5% conversion on gated content
- Landing pages: 10-25% conversion rate
- Webinars: 40-50% show rate
